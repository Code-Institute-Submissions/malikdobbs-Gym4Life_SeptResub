How we ensure your safety....

Social Distancing

No need to worry about being to close to other members whilst working out or feeling unsafe. We have floor markers on the gym floor, studio and cycle classrooms.

Sanitising/Cleaning Stations

Multiple sanitising and cleaning stations all around the gym floor to help keep our gym clean and hygenic. Multiple sanitizer stations available and easily located within our gym.

Extensive Cleaning

Regular cleaning throughout the day and overnight deep cleaning. Staff are rota'd to clean the gym floor every hour including changing rooms, toilets, studio and gym equipment.


Hygeine

Good hand hygeine helps keep viruses at bay. Multiple hand sanitizing stations located around our gym to help minimize spreads and infections.
