Strength Classes

Improve the shape of your body by increasing your muscle mass with strength & conditioning. This class is for you if your looking to tone, define or stengthen your muscles.


Pump


Tone & build muscle mass, burn fat and lose calories. Pump classes involve weights and will give your whole body a workout. Variety of exercises to workout your whole body such as squats, deadlifts, pushups, chest presses, lunges plus many more.


Class Duration: 60 minutes

Tone


Tone classes aim to both build muscle as well as burn calories due to both aerobic exercise (fitness for your heart) and resistance training (for strength and stability).




Class Duration: 45 minutes


Legs, Bums & Tums (L.B.T.)


Tone muscle and burn fat as you lunge, squat, step your way to shape. Using both weights and your own bodyweight. The intensity and repitions you complete will put your lower body through their paces.


Class Duration: 45 minutes

Ab Blaster


Abs class aims to burn fat and build muscle in the area we all love to hate! Twist, crunch and plank your way through this class to help either flatten your tummy or build your amazing 6 pack.


Class Duration: 15 minutes
