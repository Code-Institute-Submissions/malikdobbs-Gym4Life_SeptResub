Mind & Body Classes

Improve your flexibility, durability, core strength and both your physical and mental wellbeing.

Strech classes are stimulating and improve both your flexibility and durability of your muscles. This class will also improve your core strength and balance. Stretch is suitable for both beginners and experienced members.

Yoga


Yoga classes are stimulating for both the mind and body through controlled breathing to help regulate your physical and mental wellbeing. Yoga will also improve your strength and tone the whole body with yoga postures.

Pilates is a low impact exercise class that strengthens not just your abdominal muscles, but also your full body. This class also strengthens yourcore and improves both your flexibility and posture.

Body Balance


A yoga inspired class that will improve your mind as well as your body. Body Balance also, has elements of Pilates and Tai Chi that will get you stretching to help improve flexibilty. Body Balance is suitable for beginners and experienced members.


Class Duration: 30 minutes
