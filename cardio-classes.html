Cardio


Cardio Classes

Get Your Heart Rate Up, Get fitter and burn some calories whilst listening to vibrant music that gets you pumped.


Spin


Spin is one of our most popular classes. Ride along on your watt bike to exciting, vibrant and motivating music. Whilst getting fitter and burning calories you will improve your endurance, lose weight and strengthen both your core and legs getting a full body workout in.


Class Duration: 30 minutes

Circuit


Prepare for a quick paced, fun and energetic classs. Workouts will consist of you moving from station to station, performing different exercises. This class is designed to improve your fitness, strength, power and endurance.


Class Duration: 45 minutes


Zumba


Have fun dancing to latin rhythmitic music. This cardio class is designed to improve your cardiovascular and fitness with interval training.


Class Duration: 30 minutes

Boxercise


In our non contact boxing class you will be hitting pads, practicing boxing combinations, as well as completing both cardio and bodyweight exercises such as skipping, running, pushups and more.


Class Duration: 45 minutes


Step


Step is a fun, choreographed cardio class which only requires you and your step box. This class will melt calories and improve your fitness whilst listening to good music. Both beginners and advanced users can attend this as both the PT and other members attending will guide you through the class if you can't quite keep up.


Class Duration: 30 minutes

Bootcamp


Bootcamp is a cardio based class where you will be working at high intensity with short intervals consisting of 30 seconds on and 30 seconds rest. This class will get your heart rate up, burn fat and tone your muscles.


Class Duration: 45 minutes
